This is the word list in order of frequency.
The frequency of a word is the number of times it appears in the text.
In the frequency list, words are sorted from the highest to the lowest frequency.

The words are also divided into frequency groups.
For example, the group "20-15" lists all the words with a frequency between 20 and 15.
Each word is linked to its concordance. (See the directions on concordances)

See also the statistics by frequency.

To search for the words with a frequency, e.g. of 18:
  • click on the group in the list above containing that frequency: e.g. "20-15"
  • browse the list. Words are sorted by descending frequency.
